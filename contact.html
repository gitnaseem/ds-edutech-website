Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course? +

Simply sign up on our website, browse through the available courses, and click "Enroll Now" on your chosen course. You can start learning immediately after enrollment.

Do you offer refunds? +

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the course, you can request a full refund within 7 days of enrollment, no questions asked.

Can I access courses offline? +

Yes! You can download course materials and watch videos offline with our mobile app. This ensures you can learn anywhere, anytime, without internet dependency.

How does the doubt clearing system work? +

You can post your doubts on our platform. Our expert instructors will respond within 24 hours. We also conduct weekly live doubt clearing sessions where you can ask questions in real-time.

What boards and streams do you cover? +

We provide comprehensive coverage for CBSE and 18+ State Boards. Our courses cover Science, Arts, and Commerce streams for classes VI to XII.

Do you provide certificates? +

Yes, upon completing a course and passing the final exam, you'll receive a digital certificate of completion from DS_EDUTECH that you can share on your resume or social profiles.
